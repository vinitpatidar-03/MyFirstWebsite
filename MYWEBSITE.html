Owner Info

youtube

Steve Chen

YouTube, Web site for sharing videos. It was registered on February 14, 2005, by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim, three former employees of the American e-commerce company PayPal.

Instagram

Kevin Systrom

Kevin Systrom is an American computer programmer and entrepreneur. He co-founded Instagram, the world's largest photo sharing website, along with Mike Krieger. Systrom was included on the list of America's Richest Entrepreneurs Under 40 2016.

facebook

Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg is famous for being cofounder and chief executive officer of Facebook, the world's largest social network Web site. He founded the service in 2004 while he was at Harvard University with four of his fellow students.

twitter

Jack Dorsey

Twitter was created by Jack Dorsey, Noah Glass, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams in March 2006 and launched in July of that year. By 2012, more than 100 million users posted 340 million tweets a day, and the service handled an average of 1.6 billion search queries per day.
